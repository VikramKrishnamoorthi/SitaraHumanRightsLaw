Leading Immigrant Outreach and Rights Advocacy

I have been recognized by the San Diego County Bar Association for founding the Afghan Outreach Project to support resettled legal professionals from Afghanistan. Today, I continue this work as a board member of the Afghan Refugee Empowerment Fund. I have also launched a research project aimed at centering Afghan refugees in human rights policymaking.

Currently representing ICE detainees in federal habeas proceedings (S.D. Cal.)
Currently support a refugee-led research project examining Taliban custodial torture in Afghanistan.
Previously supported expert discovery in pending class action litigation addressing conditions of detention in a private ICE detention facility.

Disclaimer:

I am not an immigration lawyer and cannot help with immigration cases before USCIS, immigration court, or the BIA. But if you are an immigrant or refugee and would like me to investigate a systemic practice by ICE violating noncitizens’ constitutional rights, please contact me. I additionally support nonprofit immigration service providers in San Diego with current habeas needs.
